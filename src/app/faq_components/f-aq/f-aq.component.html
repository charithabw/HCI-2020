Frequently Asked Questions

Buyer-Purchasing

How do I buy a book?
  • Step 1

    • Select any text book that you prefer, Click add it to cart & Select proceed to checkout.

  • Step 2

    • Fill the form, click submit.

  • Step 3

    • Select a payment method from any options given below (Ex- credit card, direct bank deposit , Ez cash) & submit.

Do I have to be a member to buy a book?
  • NO, you don’t have to be a member to purchase a book.

What are the benefits of becoming a member?
  • You can sell a book as an individual seller only if you become a member and also be added to our promotions & discounts./li>

Do you accept orders over the phone?
  • No you can't,But you can call & place an order with the help of our online assistants.

What kind of payments do you accept?

    We accept three methods of payments.

  • Direct bank deposits
  • Credit cards
  • Ez cash

What measures have been taken to protect my payments & personal information?
  • We offer the use of a secure server. All supplied sensitive/credit information is transmitted via Secure Socket Layer (SSL) technology and then encrypted into our Payment Gateway providers database only to be accessible by those authorized with special access rights to such systems, and are required to keep the information confidential.

How can I get Special offers & discounts?
  • By registering yourself as a Member you can enjoy our Promotions & Discounts.

How do I change my delivery address?
  • If you are a member, you can log in to your account from the menu & click on “my account details”. Then click on “edit” & include your delivery address. Finally click on “update” for immediate updating of delivery address.

What if I can’t find the Book I’m looking for?
  • You can go to our Help page & send us a direct email regarding your book, we will help you to figure out your book problem with an ease. Also, if we haven’t got the book you are looking for, we can “special order” the requested book just for you.

ReSeller-Selling

How to sell my books?
  • Step 1

    • Register as a MEMBER and you will have the privilege of selling your Used/New books or if you are a bookshop & want to sell books by your own company name register in “Book Shop”.

  • Step 2

    • Fill in the form with your own password & submit. An e-mail will be delivered to your given e- mail account confirming your account at InternationalSchoolBooks.lk.

  • Step 3

    • After the confirmation, you can sign in with your email & password, go to “My Account”, select “Add your book to sell” then fill the form adding your book details and select your book from our database or add your own book (which has been approved by the ADMINISTARTOR) and then your book will be added.

Can I sell used books at InternationalSchoolBooks.lk?
  • Yes, you can sell all your used but readable books with its Real condition clearly mentioned.

How will I show the book condition?
  • When you add details of your book select, Book condition as “used” and fill in the rest of the fields.

How do you pay me?
  • When a book/s has been purchased by a customer an e-mail will be sent. Payment will only be sent after 14 days by a cheque incase the customer disputes the condition of the book than what has been described on the site.

What measures have been taken to protect my payments & sales?
  • We offer the use of a secure server. All supplied sensitive/credit information is transmitted via Secure Socket Layer (SSL) technology and then encrypted into our Payment gateway providers database only to be accessible by those authorized with special access rights to such systems, and are required to keep the information confidential.

How does your ranking of selling books work?
  • Your books would be ranked according to key factors such as price, quality of book, customer reviews of your previous sales.

Book Shops

Selling a book
  • Step 1

    • Sign in as a Bookshop.

  • Step 2

    • Fill in the valid form with your own password & submit. An e-mail will be delivered to your given e- mail account which will confirm your account with InternationalSchoolBooks.lk.

  • Step 3

    • After the confirmation and signing in with your e-mail & password, Go to “My account” select “Add your book to sell”. Fill in the form including the book details and select your book from our database or add your own book (which has been approved by the ADMINISTRATOR). Now your book will be added to be sold under the book title mentioned in your form.

How will I create an account to buy a book?
  • Go to sign in , fill your e-mail , select Book shop under " Do you have an InternationalSchoolBooks.lk password?" and submit. You will be asked to fill your details in the next page. Please fill your details and continue with your registration following the steps given in InternationalSchoolBooks.lk website.

How do you pay me?
  • When a book has been purchased by a customer you will receive an e-mail informing the purchase of your book. You will be contacted soon by our accounts division asking your bank details for us to make the payment for your sold book.

How do you charge me?
  • Since you are a Book shop & have a “Book Shop” account in our InternationalScholBooks.lk web site , we will charge 30 % on each book sale.

What measures has been taken to protect my payments & sales?
  • We offer the use of a secure server. All supplied sensitive/credit information is transmitted via Secure Socket Layer (SSL) technology and then encrypted into our Payment gateway providers database only to be accessible by those authorized with special access rights to such systems, and are required to Keep the information confidential.

How does your ranking of selling books work?
  • Your books would be ranked according to key factors such as price, quality of book, customer reviews of your previous sales.

How will I know if my book has been sold?
  • When a customer purchases your book, our system will automatically send you an e-mail informing you the sale of the specified book & Details of delivering the book/s to us. If a customer cancels the book order, you will also be notified with an e-mail & your book automatically will be added in to the selling book list.
